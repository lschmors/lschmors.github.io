As PhD students we often get asked, *"Hey, how is your thesis going?"* This question can often create a lot of pressure even if it is well-intended. I had the great pleasure to discuss this topic with two of the authors of a paper published in 2023 about the mental health of PhD students.

Julian Friedrich and Markus Kleinhansl are part of the PhD initiative [Sustainability](https://www.sustainability-phd.com/) in T√ºbingen, which has actively engaged in various sustainability projects. They also created a survey focusing on the working habits and mental health of PhD students. Among their findings: 31.3% of participants work 45 hours or more, and one third of PhD students is at risk of major depression.

During the podcast, we discuss the causes of stress among PhD students and how this pressure can sometimes lead to crisis situations. We also talk about practical techniques to manage stress and overcome the "imposter" feeling many of us experience.
